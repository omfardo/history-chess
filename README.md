## 🎮 ABOUT

ChronoChess is a cinematic chess experience where players command legendary civilizations across the ages. Battle with museum-quality pieces on weathered stone boards in an atmosphere of epic grandeur.

### **Current Version: 0.1 MVP**
- 2 Civilizations: Roma 🏛️ & Egypt 🔺
- Classic chess rules
- Cinematic visuals (AAA quality)
- Responsive design (desktop + mobile)

---

## ✨ FEATURES

### **Gameplay:**
- ✅ Full chess rules (pawn, rook, knight, bishop, queen, king)
- ✅ Move validation & turn system
- ✅ Unicode & SVG piece sets
- ⏳ AI opponent (coming soon)
- ⏳ Draw detection (coming soon)
- ⏳ Move history & undo (coming soon)

### **Visuals:**
- ✅ Cinematic hero section
- ✅ Epic CSS effects (shadows, glows, animations)
- ✅ Weathered stone textures (SPQR + Hieroglyphs)
- ✅ Arena background (Colosseum atmosphere)
- ✅ Hover effects (piece lift + golden glow)
- ✅ Smooth animations
